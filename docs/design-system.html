Clean Shopper

Design System v1.0 · April 4, 2026

Trustworthy, approachable, and grounded in a wellness sensibility. The visual language communicates credibility through generous spacing, muted natural color, and clean typography — like a knowledgeable friend who happens to be an ingredient expert.

Design Principles

Trust through clarity

Every element earns its place. Clean layouts, honest color, and readable typography signal that the information here is reliable.

Warm expertise

The interface feels like getting advice from someone who genuinely cares. Warm tones and generous spacing prevent ingredient analysis from feeling clinical.

Generous breathing room

White space is a feature. Spacious layouts reduce cognitive load and let users focus on what matters — assessments, comparisons, and recommendations.

Quiet confidence

The design doesn't shout. Hierarchy is established through spacing and weight rather than size and saturation. Credibility comes from substance.

Surface Hierarchy

Page Background — sand-100

Card Surface — white

Typography

Poppins throughout — modern and friendly, with enough weight range to create clear hierarchy without a second typeface. Geometric construction reads as precise; rounded terminals read as warm.
